# Privacy Policy

SockSwitch does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information.

All user settings (SSH host and port number) are stored locally on your device using the browser's built-in storage API. No data is sent to any external server.

This extension requires the following permissions:

- **Proxy** — to configure browser proxy settings on your device
- **Storage** — to save your preferences locally
- **Localhost access** — to detect if your SSH tunnel is running

No analytics, tracking, or telemetry of any kind is included.
